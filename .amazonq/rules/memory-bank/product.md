# Maak Health - Product Overview

## Project Purpose
Maak Health is a comprehensive mobile health application built with React Native and Expo that provides personalized health monitoring, family health management, and AI-powered health assistance. The app focuses on proactive health management through continuous monitoring, fall detection, and intelligent health insights.

## Key Features & Capabilities

### Health Monitoring & Tracking
- **Vital Signs Monitoring**: Real-time PPG (photoplethysmography) heart rate measurement using camera
- **Health Data Integration**: Apple HealthKit and Google Health Connect synchronization
- **Comprehensive Tracking**: Medications, symptoms, moods, vitals, and medical history
- **Fall Detection**: Motion sensor-based fall detection with emergency contact alerts
- **Health Metrics**: Blood pressure, heart rate, oxygen saturation, sleep, steps, and more

### AI-Powered Health Assistant
- **Zeina AI**: Premium AI health assistant with contextual health insights
- **Personalized Recommendations**: AI-driven health advice based on user data
- **Chat Interface**: Natural language interaction for health queries and guidance
- **Health Context Integration**: AI assistant with access to complete health profile

### Family Health Management
- **Family Sharing**: Multi-user health data sharing and monitoring
- **Emergency Contacts**: Automatic fall detection alerts to family members
- **Collaborative Care**: Shared medication reminders and health tracking
- **Privacy Controls**: Granular permissions for family data sharing

### Premium Features & Monetization
- **RevenueCat Integration**: Subscription-based premium features
- **Feature Gating**: Tiered access to advanced health monitoring
- **Premium AI**: Enhanced AI capabilities for paying subscribers
- **Advanced Analytics**: Detailed health insights and trend analysis

## Target Users

### Primary Users
- **Health-Conscious Individuals**: People actively monitoring their wellness
- **Chronic Condition Patients**: Users managing ongoing health conditions
- **Elderly Users**: Seniors requiring fall detection and family monitoring
- **Caregivers**: Family members monitoring loved ones' health

### Use Cases
- **Daily Health Tracking**: Continuous monitoring of vitals and symptoms
- **Medication Management**: Reminders and adherence tracking
- **Emergency Response**: Fall detection and automatic alert system
- **Health Insights**: AI-powered analysis of health trends and patterns
- **Family Coordination**: Shared health information for collaborative care
- **Clinical Integration**: Export health data for healthcare providers

## Value Proposition
Maak Health combines advanced health monitoring technology with AI-powered insights to provide a comprehensive health management platform that keeps users and their families connected, informed, and proactive about their health and safety.
