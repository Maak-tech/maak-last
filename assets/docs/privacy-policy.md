# Maak Privacy Policy

*Last Updated: December 2024*

## Introduction

We at Maak ("we," "our," or "us") are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal and health information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("Maak App" or "the App").

By using the Maak App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use the App.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- Name and contact information
- Email address
- Phone number
- Date of birth
- Gender
- Profile information

### Health Information
- Health metrics and data from Apple Health/Google Fit
- Fitness tracking data
- Medical history (if provided)
- Health goals and preferences
- Medication reminders and adherence data

### Technical Information
- Device information (model, operating system)
- App usage analytics
- Error logs and crash reports
- IP address
- Location data (with your consent)

### Usage Information
- Features used within the App
- Time spent in the App
- User interactions and behaviors
- Preferences and settings

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

### Primary Services
- Providing personalized health tracking and insights
- Delivering medication reminders and health notifications
- Synchronizing with health platforms (Apple Health, Google Fit)
- Generating health reports and analytics

### App Improvement
- Improving app functionality and user experience
- Developing new features
- Analyzing usage patterns
- Troubleshooting technical issues

### Communication
- Sending important app updates
- Providing customer support
- Sending health-related tips and information
- Notifying about policy changes

### Legal and Safety
- Complying with legal obligations
- Protecting user safety
- Preventing fraud and abuse
- Enforcing our terms of service

### Service Providers

### Health Platform Integration

- Google Fit (Android)

### Legal Requirements

## Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information:

### Technical Safeguards
- End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
- Secure data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
- Regular security audits and updates
- Multi-factor authentication options

### Access Controls
- Limited employee access to personal data
- Regular access reviews
- Secure authentication systems
- Data minimization practices

### Storage Security
- Encrypted cloud storage
- Regular backups
- Secure data centers
- Compliance with industry standards

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy:
