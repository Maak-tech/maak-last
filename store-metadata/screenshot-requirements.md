## Screenshot Content Guidelines

### Text Overlay Requirements
- **Clear, readable fonts** (minimum 24pt for mobile)
- **High contrast** (dark text on light backgrounds or vice versa)
- **Benefit-focused headlines** (not feature lists)
- **Localized text** for each market

### Suggested Headlines for Screenshots:
1. "Never Miss Your Medications Again"
2. "Keep Your Family Connected & Informed"  
3. "Automatic Fall Detection Keeps You Safe"
4. "Get Instant Health Answers with AI"
5. "Track Your Health Progress Over Time"

### Visual Requirements
- **High resolution** (no pixelated elements)
- **Actual app UI** (not mockups or concept designs)
- **Realistic data** (not Lorem ipsum or placeholder text)
- **Consistent branding** (use app colors and fonts)
- **Status bar** should show full signal, battery, and time (like 9:41 AM)

### Device Frames
- Use official Apple/Google device frames
- Ensure screenshots fit perfectly without stretching
- Consider device-specific features (notch, rounded corners)

## Quality Checklist
- [ ] All screenshots are high resolution and sharp
- [ ] Text is clearly readable on mobile devices
- [ ] App UI looks native and polished
- [ ] No placeholder or test data visible
- [ ] Consistent visual style across all screenshots
- [ ] Headlines focus on user benefits, not features
- [ ] Screenshots tell a story about the app's value
- [ ] All required sizes have been created
- [ ] Files are named according to convention
- [ ] Screenshots have been tested on actual devices

## App Store Optimization Tips
- **First screenshot is most important** - it appears in search results
- **Show the app in action** - not just static screens
- **Include diverse users** in family/social features
- **Use captions strategically** - they're searchable on iOS
- **A/B test different versions** if possible
- **Update seasonally** to maintain freshness
